    Emerging trends to small businesses in 2018  

    • By 7016602538
    • •
    • 29 Aug, 2018
    • •

    Top trends related to point of sale and business

    Get ready!...You asked for it America. I am here to share the TOP Emerging trends of
    2018. We are not in the 1980’s anymore! Donald Trump is president and business is booming.
    Technology is available to everyone for minimal cost! Unemployment is at its lowest. For your
    business to survive this revolution, keeping up with trends is imperative. Here are some new
    trends in 2018.

    Artificial Intelligence(AI)
    AI lets you transform random data into beneficial research on customers habits
    and decisions.Historically, big-tech shifts have been a disadvantage for for small
    businesses and a win for large companies. Now, researchers believe small
    businesses will be ahead of the game with AI. Top benefits of AI include “freeing
    up time to get more work done” and “customer service”.

    Machine learning(ML)

    Modern machine learning detects patterns in behavior and is good at it.
    Machine Learning(ML) also markets your products to anyone on large
    platforms that have a lot of traffic. Lastly, provides a ton of tools for a
    small business to utilize and increase cost while minimizing price surges.

    Financial transactions on cell phones

    Financial exchanges on phones will continue to rise as new technology
    develops . Phones will be able to recognize your face and have virtual
    decisions. Hackers and scammers will be out to get your information.
    Security for small business will play a bigger factor in 2019.

    Cryptocurrency

    Blockchain will eventually be used in every business worldwide. The
    thought of universal currency continues to flatter everyone but Wallstreet.
    Expect that to change in 2019. Crypto is not a fad. You only feel this way
    because Bitcoin is down for the 900th time in 10+ years. Cryptocurrency
    is not going away anytime soon. Make sure your business is prepared to
    transition when blockchain becomes mainstream.

    Millennials are the big dollar focus $$$.

    Soon, millennials will outnumber baby boomers. Small businesses will
    have to be upfront and honest to the millennial audience. Millenials want
    to know the honest numbers and if the company will continue progressing
    within a five year time period.

    Hyperlocal Advertising

    Smartphones know where our location is 24/7. Even, if we turn off our
    location, GOOGLE and FACEBOOK still make tons of money and abuse
    their power. Why not link relevant ads to your exact location? Traditional,
    yet personal they say! Utilizing hyperlocal advertising will be the new
    trend in 2019.

    Live streaming video content is the next BIG thing.

    Millenials are more focused on live engagement and want real interaction.
    The new iphones release next month and one is a record breaking size.
    With that being said, 2019 could bring the mass adoption of 5G and has
    the potential of putting unlimited data on your phone. Phones users will
    never lose signal again wherever they go. 5G will never issue a lag
    opening the door to AR and VR applications, in real time too. Making sure
    your business has an application and is mobile-friendly will be critical in
    2019.

    Lead management ability.

    Marketing firms are digging up more to generate
    leads. Businesses are now developing leads easier than ever thanks to
    information provided by marketing firms. Thus, making it easy for small owners to
    have warm leads to help grow their business. 2019 will be the year of the leads
    for small businesses.
